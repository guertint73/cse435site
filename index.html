Forgotten Bands

Despite enjoying a recent comeback with the 20th anniversary Fantastic Planet tour, Failure remains a misunderstood band. Formed in the early 90's, they were grouped in with the same realm of 'alternative' bands that included contemporaries like Soundgarden and Pearl Jam. However, Failure differentiated themselves through a more textural and expansive sound. Read more here at: https://www.allmusic.com/album/magnified-mw0000623268

Bandcamp Artist of the Week

I first found Voyage whilst exploring Bandcamp for artists similiar to Home. Like Home, Voyage is an electronic artists whose atmospheric style sits somewhere in the middle between the soft warm synths of 1970's and the soundtracks of 80's reboots like "Stranger Things", all the while drenched in caverns of spatial effects. Sometimes these artists are looped in with other so-called 'Vaporwave' artists (artists whose music can sometimes be seen as a tribute to 80's and 90's instrumental soundtracks and other electronic music), but perhaps you should decide for yourself. Listen to Voyage here: https://voyage95.bandcamp.com/track/paradise
